Our Story

About Us

Our Heritage

Building Excellence Since 2016

Founded in 2016 by brothers Marcus and David BassyDue, our company began with a simple mission: to deliver construction projects that combine architectural beauty with structural integrity. What started as a small residential remodeling firm in Los Angeles has grown into a premier construction and engineering company serving clients across the United States.

Over the past 15 years, we have completed over 50 projects ranging from luxury residential estates to commercial high-rises, healthcare facilities, and sustainable mixed-use developments. Our growth has been driven by an unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and client satisfaction.

Today, BassyDue Construction & Engineering Ltd employs over 200 skilled professionals, including licensed architects, structural engineers, project managers, and master craftsmen. We maintain offices in Los Angeles, Miami, and New York, allowing us to serve clients nationwide with local expertise and personalized attention.
